Volunteer with Us

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and make a difference in your community. Whether you want to help with food collection, sorting, distribution, or administrative tasks, we have a role for you.

Why Volunteer?

  • Make a positive impact in your community
  • Meet new people and make new friends
  • Gain valuable experience and skills
  • Help reduce food waste and fight hunger

Volunteer Roles

  • Food Collection: Pick up food donations from various locations
  • Food Sorting: Sort and pack food donations for distribution
  • Food Distribution: Help distribute food to those in need
  • Administrative Support: Assist with office tasks and data entry
  • Event Support: Help organize and run our events

Volunteer Requirements

  • Must be at least 18 years old (or accompanied by a parent/guardian if under 18)
  • Must be able to commit to at least 2 hours per week
  • Must be reliable and punctual
  • Must complete a brief orientation and training session
